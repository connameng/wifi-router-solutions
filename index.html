Who we are

We are ex Cisco Systems and AT&T engineers and want to make the process of purchasing a wireless router much easier! Our pain was instantly felt after spending literally hours researching the latest technologies and “best buyer guides” for wireless routers. At that point in time, it was clear there should be a better and easier way to determine which wireless routers we should purchase, without overpaying for features we don’t need.

That’s where this project came about - to help solve this pain point. We thought it would be a cool project to do together that could provide some actual value to people's lives.
