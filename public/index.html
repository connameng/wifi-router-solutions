Who we are

We are ex-Cisco Systems and AT&T engineers and want to make the process of purchasing a wireless router much easier. Our pain was instantly felt after spending literal hours researching the latest technologies and “best buyer guides” for wireless routers. At that point in time, it was clear there should be a better and easier way to know which router was best for us, without overpaying for features we don’t need.

That’s where this project came about - to help solve this pain point. We also thought it would be a cool project to do together that could provide some actual value in people's lives. If you found this helpful please share this link with your friends, family (parents specifically) or anyone you know looking to purchase a wireless router in the near future. If you have feedback, please shoot us an email below. We hope you found this useful and enjoy!

How it works

We convert your answers to simple questions and map them to technical features and functionalities which filter out routers that do not fit your needs. We have done months of research to provide you with the absolute best selection and recommendation of wireless routers that fit your personal needs.
